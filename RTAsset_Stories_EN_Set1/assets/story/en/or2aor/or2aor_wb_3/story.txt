{The Roach and the Ants} {Project LISTEN} {WORD}


The roach is big.





Not the ants.





The ants are short.





The ants flee the roach.





Flee means run away from something. 





The ants flee to the fort.





The roach runs to the fort.





Shut the fort!





The moon is up.





The roach sleeps.





The ants wait.





It is morning.





The ants hear a storm.





Run!





The ants run.





The storm gets the roach!





The roach must flee the storm.

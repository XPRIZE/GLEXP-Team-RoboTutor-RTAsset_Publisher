{The Hot Rod} {Project LISTEN} {WORD}


Dad had a hot rod.





It was a gift.





Dad was glad.





Glad Dad hid the ham.





Was the ham in the drift?





No. Was the ham in the lift?





No. The ham was in the hat.





It was the hat of the lad.





The lad got mad.





The lad hid the ham on the raft.





The raft slid on the dim dot.





It was hot.





The lad hit the lid.





The lid slid.





The lid slid fast.





It fit on the soft mat.

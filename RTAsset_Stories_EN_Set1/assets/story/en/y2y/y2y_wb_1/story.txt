{Jon and the Wig} {Project LISTEN} {WORD}


Jon has a yellow wig.





The wig has a twist.





Jon can yank the twist.





Yank yank!





The yank made Jon fret.





Jon said, "can you yank my wig?"





Ron said, "Yes, I can yank your wig."





Jon said, "do not yank it yet."





Yank yank!





The yank made Jon mad!





"Do not yank it yet!"





But Ron did yank the wig.

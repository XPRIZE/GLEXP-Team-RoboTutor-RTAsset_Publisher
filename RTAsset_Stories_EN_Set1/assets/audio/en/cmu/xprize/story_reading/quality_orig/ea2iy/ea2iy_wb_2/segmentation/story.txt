The car is teal.





Teal means blue-green.





Mark got in the car.





He sat in the seat.





Then he got up.





Mark left the car.





Next, Mark had to clean the car.





Mark got water and soap.





The water did not leak. 





Is the car clean?





Yes!





Mark got in the car.





Mark went east.





And Mark went west.





Mark said, "this clean car is best!"

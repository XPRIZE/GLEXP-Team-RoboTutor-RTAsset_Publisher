{Toad Meets Bee} {Project LISTEN} {WORD}


The toad went to meet the bee.





He went on a train.





The toad had to meet the bee at the tree.





The toad had to wait.





Then he felt rain.





The rain was faint.





Then the rain was hard.





The toad sat in the rain.





Did the rain stop?





It went in the drain.





At last, the bee got to the tree.

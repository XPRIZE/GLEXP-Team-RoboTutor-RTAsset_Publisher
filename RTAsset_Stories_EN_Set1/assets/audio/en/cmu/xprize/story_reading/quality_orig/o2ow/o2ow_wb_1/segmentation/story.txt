Tom dug a hole.





A big, wide hole.





What did Tom put in the hole?





Tom put a pole in the hole.





Tom put a can of coke in the hole.





Tom put a stone in the hole.





Tom put a note in the hole.





What did the note say?





The note said, "Tom's Hole.





Do not take the pole, the coke, or the stone."





But Mike stole the stone.





And Mike stole the pole.





And Mike stole the coke.





It made Tom mad.





Mike put the pole in the hole.





Mike put the coke in the hole.





And Mike put the stone in the hole.





Mike said, "Sorry, Tom."





Tom said, "Let's jump in the hole!"

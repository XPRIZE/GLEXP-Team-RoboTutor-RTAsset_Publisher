{A Greedy Boy} {Tukei A Cathy} {1}


One day three boys from Mugulu village went to hunt.
They used dogs to help them.





When they were moving they came across two guinea fowls.
They chased them until they killed them.





When it was time to go back home, they wanted to share the birds.
One boy ran away with the two birds.





The other two boys chased him and took the birds from him.

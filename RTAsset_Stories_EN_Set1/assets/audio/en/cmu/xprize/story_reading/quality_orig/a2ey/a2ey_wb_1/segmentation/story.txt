My name is Joe.





I love cake!





Cake makes me glad.





I had a big cake.





My mom gave the cake to me.





But I ate it!





I did save a bit.





But I ate that, too!





My mom can bake lots of cakes.





She can bake hot cakes.





She can bake mint cakes.





She can bake red cakes.





My mom can bake the best cakes.





She made me ten cakes!





Ten cakes makes me glad.





No cakes makes me sad.





I love the taste of cake!

The frog slid in the mud.





It can not swim.





The frog got wet.





The frog felt its leg twist.





Dad got the wet frog.





Dad and the frog went to the vet.





Dad said, "the frog can not lift its leg."





The vet felt the wet frog.





The vet bent its leg.





The wet frog felt fit.





The frog can lift its leg!





Dad said, "You are the best vet!"





Dad and the frog left.





Dad went to bed.

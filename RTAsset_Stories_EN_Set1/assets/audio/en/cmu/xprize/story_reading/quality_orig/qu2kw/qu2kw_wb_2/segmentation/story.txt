Where is the queen?





I must ask her a question.





I have a question for the queen.





"Here I am," said the queen.





"What is your question?"





Can you hear the eal squeal?





"Not quite."





Can you feel the teal seal?





"Not quite."





Can you reach each peach?





"Not quite." The queen said, "be quiet.





No more questions."

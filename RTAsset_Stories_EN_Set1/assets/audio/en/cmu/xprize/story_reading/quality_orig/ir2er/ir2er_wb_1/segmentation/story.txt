Kirk the bird likes to play.





He sits on a twig.





First he makes a chirp.





He says, "chirp chirp!"





Can you make a bird sound?





Kirk the bird chirps and chirps.





He can make the sound with his mouth.





Chirp chirp.





He can not shout.





He can not squeak.





He can not squeal.





But he can chirp!





He can chirp loud.





Kirk sits in the fir tree.





In the fir tree he can chirp.





What a loud bird!





The end.

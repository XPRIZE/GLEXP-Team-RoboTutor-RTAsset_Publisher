{The Nut} {Project LISTEN} {WORD}


Don had a tan nut.





The nut got soft.





Did an ant find the nut?





No, a man did.





The man had a grin. 





He slit the nut.





The nut had no guts.





The man fit the nut in his hat!





The hat sat in the sun.





It got hot.





Don got mad at the man.





The man had fun.





At last, the man got a fan.





Don was glad.





Don and the man sat in the sun in the sand and got a tan!

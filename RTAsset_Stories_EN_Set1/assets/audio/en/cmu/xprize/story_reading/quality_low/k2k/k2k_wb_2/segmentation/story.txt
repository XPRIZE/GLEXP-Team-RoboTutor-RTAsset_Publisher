Ron the ram has tusks.





Ron has a hat on his tusks.





The hat is soft and tan.





The tusks can cut a nut.





Clink!





Ron the ram is in the soft sand.





Ron sinks in the sand!





His skin sinks.





His hand sinks.





His tusks sink.





His hat did not sink.





A man got the hat.





The man lifts Ron!





Ron the ram is not in the sand.

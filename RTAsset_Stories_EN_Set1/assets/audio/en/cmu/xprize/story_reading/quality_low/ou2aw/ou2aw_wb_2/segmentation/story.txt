{Fishing For Trout} {Project LISTEN} {WORD}


Trout is a fish.





Trout swim in lakes.





Do you like to fish?





Jay and Ray go fishing.





They look for trout.





"I found a good spot," said Ray.





"Do not shout," said Jay.





"It will scare the trout.





Be soft, not loud."





Jay and Ray sat.





They sat on the ground.





They had a fishing pole.





The pole had a hook on the end.





They put bait on the hook.





Did any trout bite the bait?





The hook got a mouth.





"We got one," said Jay.





"Ouch," said the trout.





"Get that out of my mouth!"





Jay and Ray let the trout go.





The end.

{Shawn Has to Yawn} {Project LISTEN} {WORD}


Shawn had to wake up.





It was dawn.





"Dawn" means the sun just came up.





Shawn had to wake up at dawn.





It made him yawn.





He had to stretch his arm.





And stretch his leg.





And yawn again.





Shawn had to feed his pet.





He had a pet hawk.





He went to the barn.





He fed the hawk.





Then he had to cut the lawn.





They had a big lawn.





It made Shawn yawn again.





The end.
